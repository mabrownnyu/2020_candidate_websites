Our nation was founded on the values and principles of putting service above self — rejecting the rule of kings who prospered from the sacrifices of the people, and forming a new nation founded on the premise that leaders should be motivated not by serving their own selfish interests — but by a desire to serve the people, a government of, by, and for the people.

But that’s not what we have today.

Instead we have a government of, by, and for the rich and powerful. This must end

The White House must be a beacon of light — respect, love and compassion

Please join me in ushering in a new century, of peace and prosperity, where we all have clean air to breathe, water to drink, and food to eat; where we’re all treated equally, where are all treated equally and have respect for each other regardless of our race, nationality, how we worship or who we love; a century that will fulfill the promise of America, of freedom, justice, equality and opportunity for all.
