ROUNDUP: Gillibrand Bursts Out of the Gate in Race for President

January 16, 2019

Last night, Senator Kirsten Gillibrand announced her exploratory committee for President of the United State on The Late Show with Stephen Colbert.  Gillibrand discussed her motivation for running, her vision for the country, her track record of fighting for women and their families, and her values of compassion, courage and determination.

Just a few minutes later, the announcement was trending in the United States.
