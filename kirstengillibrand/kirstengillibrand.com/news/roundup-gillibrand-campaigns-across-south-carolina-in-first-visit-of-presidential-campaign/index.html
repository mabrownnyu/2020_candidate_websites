ROUNDUP: Gillibrand Campaigns Across South Carolina in First Visit of Presidential Campaign

February 11, 2019

After visiting Iowa and New Hampshire, Senator Kirsten Gillibrand visited South Carolina for the first time this past weekend to listen to voters and share her vision for an American future where everyone has health care, public education is strong across every community, hard work is rewarded and the corrupting influence of money in politics is no more.

Senator Gillibrand spoke with South Carolina voters at house parties, brunched with women community leaders, met with small business leaders, donned sneakers and a gown for the Urban League Awards Gala, and attended church in North Charleston.
