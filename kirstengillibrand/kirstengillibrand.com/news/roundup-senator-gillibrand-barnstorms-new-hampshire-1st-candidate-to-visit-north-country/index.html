ROUNDUP: Senator Gillibrand Barnstorms New Hampshire; 1st Candidate to Visit North Country

February 4, 2019

In a whirlwind three-day swing of the Granite State, Senator Kirsten Gillibrand crisscrossed the state, meeting with New Hampshire students, seniors, activists and first-in-the-nation voters to hear their concerns and share her vision of an America where healthcare is a right, where public education is strong and hard work is rewarded, and where integrity prevails in the White House.

Gillibrand visited Manchester, Nashua, Portsmouth, Durham, and became the first 2020 presidential candidate to visit the North Country when she hosted a packed Q&A session in Littleton. She even managed to squeeze in a little sledding along the way.
