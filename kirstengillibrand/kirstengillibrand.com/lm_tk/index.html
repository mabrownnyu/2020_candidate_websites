Overview

Kirsten Gillibrand has released the most comprehensive national marijuana legalization plan of any presidential candidate. Find out more about Kirsten’s plan and how you can help make it a reality below.

  • Kirsten’s legalization plan: bit.ly/KG_LM
  • Industry leader and advocate Wanda James’ take on Kirsten’s plan: bit.ly/2Zenp3T

TL;DR

  • Kirsten’s plans to legalize marijuana at the federal level would create economic opportunities nationwide.
  • No plan put forth has been more explicit about ending the federal prohibition and unleashing the full medical and economic potential of legalized marijuana.
  • Kirsten’s plan is the strongest proposal for supporting those most affected by racist drug laws and supporting women- and minority-owned small businesses.
