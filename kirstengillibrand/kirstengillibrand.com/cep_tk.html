Clean Elections Plan Toolkit

Overview

Kirsten Gillibrand released her Clean Elections Plan to combat the outsized influence of money in politics. The plan is a critical structural change that holds candidates accountable to people—not special interests. It also empowers more Americans, especially young people, women, and people of color.

Read

  • NBC News on Kirsten’s plan: nbcnews.to/2GMwkBS
  • Kirsten’s Medium Post on the Plan bit.ly/2V6YD87

TL;DR:

  • Kirsten’s plan attacks the corrupting influence of money that prevents progress on the issues voters care about—like common-sense gun reform, climate change, and lowering prescription drug costs.
  • The plan empowers Americans who don’t always have a seat on the decision-making table.
  • The plan is paid for by eliminating a taxpayer subsidy that benefits the wealthy and now everyday Americans.
