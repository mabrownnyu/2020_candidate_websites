Citizen Campaign Headquarters

Join The Network
Over the last few decades, politics in America has become a spectator sport. The average citizen is too disconnected from the process, hoping that "political people" are taking care of our democracy . We the people have become like an employer who hires someone, then just checks in every two or four years to see how they're doing. What company, and what democracy, can run like that?

We are now reaping what we have sown, too many of us having become distracted from the real work of citizenship. Corporatocracy has replaced democracy as our primary organizing principle, our government that has become little more than a system of legalized bribery, and politicians too often advocate for short-term corporate profit maximization before the health and well-being of people and planet.

Only we the people can change that. And this campaign is one of the ways how.

Twice a month, please join us for a CITIZEN CAMPAIGN call, becoming part of a network of Americans who are doing more than just supporting the Marianne Williamson for President campaign. Together we are creating a great American course-correction, and putting our democracy back on track.

Part education, part meeting with Marianne, part community building, part interview with issues experts, part civic engagement, part way to find out what’s happening with the campaign, through the CITIZEN NETWORK you can involve yourself in the the great work of making America better. Many of us have been asleep, but we are asleep no longer. We are ready to engage. We are ready to rise up. We're ready to kick ass. And we're ready to save our country.
