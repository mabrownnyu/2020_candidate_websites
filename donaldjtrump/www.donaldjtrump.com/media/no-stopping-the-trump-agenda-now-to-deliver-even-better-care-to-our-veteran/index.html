No Stopping the Trump Agenda Now to Deliver Even Better Care to our Veterans

  • Share:
  • facebook
  • twitter

NEW YORK, NY – As President Trump addresses 10,000 members of the Veterans of Foreign Wars 119th Annual Convention in Kansas City, Missouri today, Lara Trump, Senior Advisor to Donald J. Trump for President, Inc. issued the following statement.

“Anyone who knows my father-in-law knows how much he loves our veterans. As President Trump addresses the 119th VFW Annual Convention in Kansas City, Missouri today, he does so with a tremendous portfolio of policies that are already providing much better care for our veterans for the first time in decades. From improving access to healthcare through tele-health technology and online scheduling tools, to expanding suicide hotlines, to holding Veterans Affairs employees accountable to the American people, Donald Trump is providing real solutions for veterans as their greatest advocate and defender in history. There’s much work yet to be done, and with the confirmation of our new VA Secretary Robert Wilkie, approved with an 86-9 vote in the Senate yesterday, there’s no stopping the Trump agenda now to deliver even better care and services to our cherished veterans. God bless our red, white, and blue heroes.”
