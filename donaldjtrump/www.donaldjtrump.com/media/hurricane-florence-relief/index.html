Hurricane Florence Relief

  • Share:
  • facebook
  • twitter

While the flooding has subsided in areas affected by Hurricane Florence in September, the region is still in crisis, as local residents and businesses continue to recover and rebuild from the devastating storm. Lara Trump recently visited her hometown area in North Carolina to lend her support for the recovery and bring attention to the ongoing need for assistance of local food and supply distribution centers and nonprofit organizations.

A video reflecting her tour of damaged areas and her visits to distribution centers and shelters has now been released by Lara Trump with an appeal for people to support the charities on the front lines of the crisis.

Commenting on her tour and the ongoing crisis, Lara Trump said, “It’s heartbreaking to see the devastation and destruction in person. The selfless actions of relief workers is awe-inspiring, but the charities and nonprofits on the front lines of this crisis need our support and prayers more than ever.”
