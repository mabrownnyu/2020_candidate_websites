June 03, 2019

Trump Campaign wishes all Muslims a happy Eid

  • Share:
  • facebook
  • twitter

Arlington, VA - As the holy month of Ramadan ends and the Eid al Fitr 2019, or the Festival of Breaking the Fast, Donald J. Trump for President, Inc. issued the following statement.

“On this most holy of celebrations for all people of the Muslim faith, all of us at Team Trump wish you a happy Eid and blessings for this Festival of Breaking the Fast. While communities all over America and the world gather in celebratory worship, we join your prayers for all of God’s blessings. Most especially, we wish you, your families, and communities peace and prosperity, and the opportunity for all of us to achieve growing respect and understanding for all faiths as we are all united by our shared blessings from God. Eid Mubarak!”
