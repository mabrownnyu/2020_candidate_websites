Results Matter: Record Support from Grassroots for President Trump

  • Share:
  • facebook
  • twitter

New York, NY – Donald J. Trump for President, Inc. announced the campaign’s quarterly fundraising totals as reflected in its Q3 2018 report to the U.S. Federal Election Commission, submitted today.

In both direct contributions and contributions raised through its joint fundraising committees with the Republican National Committee, the Trump Campaign raised over $18 million in the Third Quarter. These totals continue a record fundraising streak for a president’s first seven quarters in office. The Trump Campaign also reported cash on hand of over $35.4 million through the quarter.

The report shows that the vast majority of contributions to President Trump’s re-election committee came from donations of $200 or less. “Low-dollar” contributions represent 97.6% of the total funds raised in the quarter. 

“After twenty months in office with unparalleled results for peace and prosperity in America, President Trump has provided the results he promised,” said Lara Trump, Senior Advisor to Donald J. Trump for President, Inc. “And grassroots America has responded to these remarkable results and the booming Trump economy with their generous support of our campaign. Now we ask these supporters and millions more like them to get out and vote in the midterms so President Trump can continue to build on his agenda with even greater success for the forgotten men and women of this great country,” she concluded.
