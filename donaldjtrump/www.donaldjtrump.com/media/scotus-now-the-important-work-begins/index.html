SCOTUS: Now the Important Work Begins

  • Share:
  • facebook
  • twitter

NEW YORK, NY – Following President Trump’s much-anticipated nomination of Judge Brett Kavanaugh as an Associate Justice of the U.S. Supreme Court, Brad Parscale, Campaign Manager for Donald J. Trump for President, Inc., issued the following statement:

“President Trump has been praised from across the political spectrum for his nomination of Judge Brett Kavanaugh, and rightly so. Judge Kavanaugh is a ‘judge’s judge’ who will serve the nation admirably and will always rule based on the original intent of the Constitution as written. But now the important work begins to get Judge Kavanaugh confirmed. The President’s adversaries in the Democrat Party leadership, which fully embrace a far-left extremist political agenda, will stop at nothing to block this confirmation. That’s why the movement to Make America Great Again will rally to support President Trump as never before to ensure that obstructionist Senate Democrats, whose opposition to Trump judicial nominees is like a three-part play in the theater of the absurd, are unsuccessful in demonizing an eminently qualified jurist. It’s time for Americans to call their Senators and insist that they support a fair hearing for President Trump’s selection of Judge Kavanaugh throughout the nomination process and ultimately vote to confirm him.
